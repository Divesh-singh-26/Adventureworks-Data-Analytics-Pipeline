# 📈 Business Insights

# Overview

This document summarizes the key business insights generated from the AdventureWorks Analytics project.

Using Python for data preparation, MySQL for data warehousing, SQL for analysis, and Power BI for visualization, the project answers important business questions related to sales performance, customer behavior, product performance, territory performance, and product returns.

---

# Business Objectives

The project aims to help decision-makers:

- Monitor overall business performance
- Identify high-performing products
- Understand customer purchasing behavior
- Compare regional sales performance
- Track product returns
- Support data-driven business decisions

---

# Sales Performance Insights

The sales analysis focuses on understanding revenue and profitability.

Key metrics include:

- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin
- Total Orders
- Total Quantity Sold

Business questions answered:

- How much revenue did the business generate?
- Is profitability improving over time?
- Which months perform best?
- Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
